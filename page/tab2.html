关于软件“防盗版”观念
keyboard_arrow_down

此站的软件大多为遵守开源协议的自由及开源软件项目，每个开源项目都有其特定的开源许可证。

开源不等于无限制使用。每种开源协议对软件的再分发、修改、商用等行为均有明确约束。若需二次分发等这些软件，请仔细阅读并严格遵守其许可证条款，以免引发法律风险或违反社区规范。

开源软件无“正版”“盗版”之分，这里指的“防盗版”是防止下载到不安全的、被修改过的软件。

当前互联网上存在大量来历不明的下载站，他们可能会恶意修改软件，为了个人隐私与设备安全，请谨慎选择下载源。

一般来说最官方最可靠的下载源为项目的GitHub仓库。

从除官方外的任何来源（包括此站）下载的文件都不能完全保证为官方原版！使用此站下载内容即视为您已理解并接受相关风险。

Most software on this site consists of free and open-source software (FOSS) projects that comply with open-source licenses. Each open-source project has its own specific open-source license.

Open-source does not equal unrestricted use. Every type of open-source license imposes clear restrictions on actions such as software redistribution, modification, and commercial use. If you need to redistribute these software products, please carefully read and strictly comply with the terms of their respective licenses to avoid legal risks or violations of community norms.

There is no distinction between "genuine" and "pirated" versions for open-source software. The term "anti-piracy" used here refers to preventing the download of unsafe, modified software.

There are numerous unverified download sites on the internet today, and they may maliciously modify software. For the sake of your personal privacy and device security, please exercise caution when choosing a download source.

Generally speaking, the most official and reliable download source for a project is its official GitHub repository.

Files downloaded from any non-official source (including this site) cannot be fully guaranteed to be the official, unaltered version! By using this site to download content, you are deemed to have understood and accepted the associated risks.
